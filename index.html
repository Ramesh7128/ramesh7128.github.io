Chettinese is a magical cuisine born when Chinese was blended with Chettinad. Kalaki & Lung Fung Soup gave birth to Kalaki Soup. Mutton Kola Urundai & Manchurian Sauce gave birth to Mutton Kola Manchurian. Chicken Podimas & Momos gave birth to Chettinad Momos. About Our Story

Chettinese was started in 2013 by the Founder & CEO, Mr.Praveen Antony. The first outlet was opened in Nelson Manickam Road, near Loyola College. Since the Combos on the menu were the favourite of Loyola students, other bachelors & office goers, eventually the brand was named “Chettinese Combo Restaurant”.

All the Combos have a piece of our soul and might be because of that, there is an incomparable value for money in the combos. Zomato recognizes us a Pocket Friendly restaurant. VFM is an inevitable word in the customer reviews. Indian Express has covered us in an article for the Innovation in cuisine and the economic combos. Not to forget, Artic Awards has chosen Chettinese as the best Fusion Restaurant in Chennai for the year 2018.

The vision of the company is to take this magical cuisine and VFM combos to every place in India and in this path-breaking journey, we would like to cultivate more entrepreneurs to be a part of this massively growing Chain. We are open for Franchising.
